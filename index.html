Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about KhaznaBox.

1. How do I book a KhaznaBox locker?

You can book a locker instantly through our mobile app. Just choose your mall, locker size, and tap to reserve.

2. Is the first hour really free?

Yes! Your first hour is completely free it's covered by the stores you shop from. After that, you pay only 10 SAR per additional hour.

3. How do I open the locker?

Once booked, you’ll receive a QR code in the app. Scan it at the station to unlock your locker automatically.

4. Where can I find KhaznaBox lockers?

KhaznaBox is available in major malls across Saudi Arabia, with more locations coming soon. Use the app to find the nearest station.

5. Is it safe to store personal items?

Absolutely. Our lockers are secure, monitored 24/7, and accessible only via your personal QR code. Your belongings are safe.

6. What can I store inside the locker?

You can store shopping bags, handbags, backpacks, and small personal items. Hazardous or prohibited items are not allowed.
